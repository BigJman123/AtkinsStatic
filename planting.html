Since partnering with Precision Planting in 1997, we've expanded our services to assist our customers with their farming needs - finding better spacing and depth to potentially improve their yields per acre.

Precision Planting's goal is to increase corn production by 12 to 16 bushels per acre. This is done through advanced technologies which improve seed spacing, depth control and enhancing seed environment.
